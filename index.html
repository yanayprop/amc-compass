How to use: Add a provider slot for each quote you've received. Pick the fee structure that matches their pricing model, fill in the numbers, and the tool calculates annual cost. Turn on "Model AUM growth" to see how costs play out over time instead of at a single AUM snapshot.

Directional only. This tool models cost drag under stated fee structures. It does not account for FX hedging, transaction costs, tax treatment, jurisdictional risk, distribution fees, or anything else material to a structuring decision. Confirm all pricing directly with providers.
